Frequently asked questions

Can Clean My Tweets review my entire tweet history?

Clean my tweets reviews as many tweets as possible, for most users that's up 3,000, though your settings may impact this.

Do I have to delete every tweet that gets flagged?

What you choose to do with the tweets we flag is up to you. You may choose to delete these tweets as they no longer align with your beliefs, or you may still stand by them and choose to leave them be.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, debit cards and Apple Pay.

Does clean my tweets check retweets and likes?

We check your retweets but not likes at this time.

How do you decide what is potentially harmful?

We do not aim to dictate what is or is not harmful, rather we air on the side of caution and identify any content that could potentially be harmful.

Do you store any data about my tweets or tweet history?

We firmly believe that the safest way to store your data, is to not keep it at all. By the end of your transaction, Clean My Tweets deletes all of your tweet text and related information from our systems.
